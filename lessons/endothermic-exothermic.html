Section 01 // Core Concept

Energy on the move

When chemicals react, bonds break and reform — and energy moves between the reaction and its surroundings. We feel that as a temperature change. Two outcomes:

EXOTHERMIC

exo = exit
Energy OUT → 🌡️ heats up

ENDOTHERMIC

endo = into
Energy IN → 🌡️ cools down

Section 06 // Encrypted: GCSE Stretch

Advanced data

🔒 Reaction profiles & energy change

The hump on the scanner is the activation energy — the push needed to start a reaction. Every reaction has one.

The vertical gap between reactants and products is the overall energy change (ΔH):

Exothermic: products lower → energy released → ΔH is negative.
Endothermic: products higher → energy absorbed → ΔH is positive.

Bond energy shortcut:

ΔH = bonds broken − bonds made

Make more than you break → negative → exothermic. Break more than you make → positive → endothermic.
